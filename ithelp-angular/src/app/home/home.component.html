Set up your profile

Sign up on our platform and set up your profile. Register as a nerd and include everything you are good at fixing. Users will look for people with certain skills so better write everything you are good at and be ready to help them.

Find people around you

Check who is around your location in our map. The map will show you users that fits your search criteria. You could also check the reviews that other users gave them. Don't worry, you will findsomebody that will know how to help you!

Book the service you need

Once you find somebody with the skills needed for your problem, you are one click away from booking him! Set the date and the hours and let him know all the details about your problem. He will receive your message and after you both agree on days and price, your problem will solved!
